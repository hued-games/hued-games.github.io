About Hued Games

Hued Games is an indie game studio dedicated to turning imagination into interactive experiences. We believe games should be more than just entertainment — they should spark curiosity, inspire creativity, and connect people through shared challenges and fun.

From casual puzzles to immersive adventures, every project we create is fueled by our love for gaming. Our design philosophy is simple: easy to learn, fun to master. Whether you’re a casual gamer or a hardcore player, we aim to craft titles that bring you back for "just one more round".

At Hued Games, every game is a new journey, and we invite you to join us in exploring endless possibilities.
