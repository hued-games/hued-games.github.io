Privacy Policy

Hued Games ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is used when you use our mobile applications ("Games" or "Services").

By downloading and using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location while you play our Games. However, to operate and improve our services and to display advertisements, some data may be collected automatically through our third-party partners.

Usage Data: This is anonymous data about how you interact with our Games. For example, data such as levels you complete, in-game clicks, and session duration is collected to improve the game experience.

Device and Ad Data: Certain technical data about your device is collected for the purpose of serving and personalizing advertisements. This data may include:

  • Your device's Advertising Identifier (ADID for Android, IDFA for iOS)
  • IP Address
  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Type of internet connection

This data may be considered personal data under legal regulations such as GDPR and CCPA.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the functionality of our Services.
  • To analyze the game experience to improve our games and fix bugs.
  • To serve relevant and personalized advertisements to our users.
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, such as:
